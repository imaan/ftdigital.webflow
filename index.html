Frequently Asked Questions

How can you deliver results equally across different types of roles?

Our unique selling point is our ability to to accelerate the process of gaining experience and confidence in applying a given framework for delivery.

For in-house skills we design our frameworks based on the way you work today, fine-tuning where necessary.

When we're training new-hires on skills you don't currently possess internally, we use frameworks designed by the biggest and best in the technology industry to guarantee their quality.

We have proven this approach to be highly effective for Product, Sales, Marketing and Engineering roles, but it can be applied to anything from learning to swim to playing poker.
How long does it take to get a new-hire performing at a high level?

It depends entirely on the complexity of the role and the number of new skills that need to be learnt.

As a rule of thumb, for junior hires 1 month is plenty, whereas senior hires can take 3 months or more.

In larger cohorts, sometimes a few members of their group who need their hand holding a little longer than others, and so we offer a heavily discounted maintenance plan to support them also.
What if they never improve?

The plans are designed very specifically around the skills that you have hired for in a way that greatly mitigates failure.

If the plan is followed to completion, and the hire has the base-skills that you are hiring for, there is very little scope for the plan failing, that is the beauty of it.
